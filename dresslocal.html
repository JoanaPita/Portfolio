Dress Local

Look through your neighbours wardrobe
for a free community based shopping experience

The Challenge

In an industry producing 92 million tonnes of waste per year the priority should be to buy less and share more. With Dress Local we can say goodbye to clothing that doesn’t serve us anymore and hello to pre-loved pieces that are looking for new homes. It’s through trading and gifting that Dress Local users travel the world of fashion, producing less waste and saving money.

Careful Branding

The choice of the main colour, green, represents renewal, sustentability and nature, green is also associated with peace, rest and security.
A new icon set was designed from scratch for this app, the lines were kept purposefully rounded to make it more inviting to the user.
