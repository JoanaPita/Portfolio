Branding for fiber arts/cross stitch brand Studio Paca

Short logo and cross stitch pattern downloadle re-design project

Studio Paca sells Cross Stitch Downloadable Patterns and the brand required a more playful design to stand apart from the standard cross stitch patterns generated by computer programs sold by the competition. A new logo was also presented to reflect the doodles the creator draws on packages.

I wanted to bring the colours and playfulness of Studio Pacaâ€™s pixel art into the graphic design of the patterns, to make it all more cohesive. The customer will feel like they are opening the page of a file bringing that tactile memory into the digital space, making it appear less like sterile white page of any other pdf pattern.

The logo was designed to appear like a stamp of approval so the costumer will identify it as an authentic and genuine product.
